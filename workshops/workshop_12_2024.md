## Location

The 2024 3D-BioInfo-PT Intermediate Workshop will take place at [Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade de Lisboa](https://ciencias.ulisboa.pt/en) (Building C8, North Wing, 5th Floor, Room 8.5.50D).

C8 Building, Campo Grande
1749-016 Lisboa  
Portugal  
### GPS coordinates

## How to get there?

The access to FCUL is quick and easy, particularly for those who arrive at the Campo Grande metro station. From there, one can walk about 500m to FCUL.
